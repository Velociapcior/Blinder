Blinder

A conversation-first blind dating app. Dignity before desirability — users are evaluated through interaction quality before visual filtering. Not a better swipe feed: a different first-impression protocol.

Voice

A thoughtful friend, not a system. Calm authority meets warm humanity. Never pressures, never blames, never performs positivity it hasn't earned. Short, specific, forward-looking.

Tone by moment

OnboardingIntrigue + quiet confidence — "this is different, you belong here."
WaitingCalm anticipation, anchored in time.
First messageCourageous warmth — acknowledge the vulnerability of going first.
Active conversationReceding — let the two humans talk.
Decision gateGrounded, explanatory, equal-weight.
Resolution waitCharged but unhurried — "your answer is with them."
Mutual revealWarm joy, shared achievement, unhurried.
Non-mutual endingGentle acceptance — "this conversation has ended."

Words

Never
  • "Rejected", "declined"
  • "They chose not to reveal"
  • "Match expired"
  • "No matches yet"
  • "Error" / "Failed" on their own
  • Exclamation marks in endings
Instead
  • "This conversation has ended."
  • Omit attribution entirely.
  • "This conversation has ended."
  • "Your match arrives daily."
  • Always pair with a human next step.
  • Full stop or no punctuation.
